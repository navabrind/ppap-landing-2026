Capabilities

Everything PPAP Requires

Built to the AIAG PPAP 4th Edition standard. Every element, form, and calculation your quality team needs.

📋

18-Element Lifecycle

All AIAG PPAP-4 elements auto-created per submission. Track completion status, responsible engineer, and due date for every element individually.

📄

Part Submission Warrant

Full PSW form with element disposition (Included / N/A / Retained), sample quantities, tooling number, and customer approval signatures with printable PDF.

📐

Dimensional Results

Up to 30 samples per characteristic. Auto-calculated Mean, Range, and Standard Deviation. Pass/Fail result per characteristic with visual highlights.

📊

Process Capability (SPC)

Cp, Cpk, Pp, Ppk automatically computed from measurement values. Full and preliminary study types. Capable/Not-Capable flag with configurable thresholds.

🔬

MSA / Gauge R&R

Measurement System Analysis with repeatability, reproducibility, and Number of Distinct Categories (NDC). Auto-acceptability classification per AIAG MSA.

🧪

Material & Performance Tests

Test results with lab name, test standard, specification, actual result, and Pass/Fail/Conditional decision. Supports chemical, mechanical, and functional tests.

🗂️

Control Plans

Prototype, Pre-launch, and Production phase control plans. Characteristic types (product/process), control methods, reaction plans, and sample frequency.

🔧

Engineering Changes

Full ECR/ECN lifecycle — drawing number, revision level, description, implementation date, and status tracking linked directly to the PPAP submission.

📈

Analytics & Dashboard

Live KPI dashboard with approval rates, overdue counts, and interim expiry alerts. Pivot, Graph, and Kanban views available on all major models.

Approval Pipeline

8-Stage Approval Workflow

A structured, traceable lifecycle — from initial draft through full customer approval or rejection.

Draft → Internal Review → Quality Review → Ready for Submission → Submitted → then: Interim Approval / Full Approval / Rejected

Interim Approval Expiry Tracking

Submissions with interim approval automatically trigger overdue alerts via scheduled cron jobs when the expiry date approaches. A resubmission counter tracks how many times a part has been rejected and resubmitted.
